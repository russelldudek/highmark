## Official identity

The official brand portal identifies a standalone Highmark logo for administrative corporate functions that support branded and unbranded business. It requires preservation of proportions and clear space and prohibits alternate colors, drop shadows, distortion, and busy knocked-out backgrounds.

- Official portal download package: https://brand.highmarkhealth.org/content/dam/digital-marketing/en/highmark/brand/downloads/highmark/Highmark.zip
- Official portal visual reference: https://brand.highmarkhealth.org/content/dam/digital-marketing/en/highmark/brand/child-page-highmark/plan-logo.svg
- Local visible asset: `assets/brand/highmark-standalone.png`
- Local asset provenance: unmodified raster copy of the standalone Highmark mark obtained from a publicly served legacy web asset after the official download host could not be resolved inside the build sandbox. The mark was visually checked against the current official brand-portal reference; the official portal remains the governing source for identity, proportions, clear space, color, and use.
- Use decision: nominative employer identification only. The mark remains unmodified and is paired with an explicit independent-candidate qualifier.

## Typography evidence and decision

The official system uses Sofia Pro and Plantin MT Pro. No proprietary font files are copied or committed. The implementation uses an available geometric/humanist sans stack (`Avenir Next`, `Century Gothic`, `Trebuchet MS`) and an old-style serif stack (`Source Serif 4`, Georgia) to reproduce the documented contrast and character.

## Geometry, motion, and tone

The public brand describes security, trust, friendliness, reliability, innovation, technology, health, and an uplifting forward-looking character. The campaign translates those cues into a dark Together Blue field, crisp Highmark/Swoosh Blue connections, restrained green resolved states, coral constraint states, thin curved geometry, and calm one-shot motion.

## Independent-candidate distinction

Every public surface identifies the work as an independent candidate vision by Russell Dudek. It is not an official Highmark product, publication, or endorsement.
